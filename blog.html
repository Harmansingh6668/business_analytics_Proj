Key Findings

Cluster 1: Budget Conscious

Low income, moderate spending score. Value-seeking customers who make calculated purchases.

Cluster 2: Premium Shoppers

High income, high spending score. Target audience for luxury and premium products.

Cluster 3: Average Consumers

Medium income, medium spending. Balanced shopping behavior.

Cluster 4: Careful Spenders

High income, low spending score. Conservative spending despite high earning capacity.

Cluster 5: Impulsive Buyers

Low income, high spending score. Prone to impulse purchases regardless of income.

Technical Implementation

from sklearn.cluster import KMeans
import pandas as pd

# Load and prepare data
df = pd.read_csv('mall_customers.csv')
X = df[['Annual_Income', 'Spending_Score']]

# Initialize and fit K-Means
kmeans = KMeans(n_clusters=5, random_state=42)
clusters = kmeans.fit_predict(X)

# Get cluster centers
centers = kmeans.cluster_centers_
